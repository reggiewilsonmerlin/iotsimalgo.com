Three steps to get started
1

Open TradingView and find the indicator

In TradingView, open any chart and click the Indicators button at the top of the screen. In the dialog that opens, switch to the Invite-only Scripts tab on the left.

You should see IOTSIMICT (or the edition you subscribed to) listed. Click it to add it to your chart.

Open TradingView ↗
2

Configure it to your preferences

Open the indicator's settings (gear icon on the chart) and walk through the input groups. Default values are illustrative, not recommendations — they exist so the indicator runs out of the box, not as a strategy you should trade.

Spend time understanding each input. Match the configuration to your own analysis, your instrument, your timeframe, and your risk tolerance. The Insight Monitor will reflect the simulation results of whatever you've configured.

3

Set your alerts (optional)

If you want to be notified when your configured criteria align on the chart, enable the alert toggles in the System & Alerts section, then create a TradingView alert in the usual way (right-click the chart → Add Alert → Condition: IOTSIM).

Alerts notify you when your criteria are met. They are not signals, recommendations, or instructions — see the Risk Disclaimer for the full position.

Common questions
I can't see the indicator in my Invite-only Scripts list

TradingView server propagation can take up to 10 minutes. Try this in order:

① Wait 5 minutes, refresh TradingView, and check the Invite-only Scripts tab again.
② If it's still missing, log out of TradingView and log back in.
③ Confirm in your Whop dashboard that you entered your TradingView username correctly (it's case-sensitive on some flows).
④ If 15+ minutes have passed and it's still not appearing, email support@iotsimalgo.com with your Whop order ID and TradingView username.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

You have full access for 14 days. No payment is taken during the trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged at all.

If you don't cancel, your first paid billing period starts automatically at the end of the trial. From that point, fees are non-refundable — see the Return & Refund Policy for the full position.

Where do I cancel if I decide IOTSIM isn't for me?

Cancel via your Whop dashboard. Cancellation during the trial means no charge is ever made. Cancellation after the trial means your subscription continues until the end of the current billing period and then stops.

Can I use the indicator on more than one TradingView account?

No — one TradingView account per subscription. This is set out in Section 4 of the Terms and is enforced by Whop's TradingView app. If you need to change which TradingView account is linked, contact support@iotsimalgo.com.

Can I automate trades with IOTSIM alerts?

For your own personal account only, yes — subject to all the conditions in Section 6b of the Terms. In short: it must be your own account, you must comply with your broker's terms and your local laws, and no alert or output can ever be exposed to anyone else.

Operating a signal service, copy-trading offering, or paid alerts channel based on IOTSIM outputs is strictly prohibited and grounds for immediate termination plus referral to TradingView, your broker, and the relevant regulator.
