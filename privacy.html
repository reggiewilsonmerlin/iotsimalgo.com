4. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your subscription and grant access to the indicator
  • To send subscription-related emails (receipts, access confirmations, renewal notices)
  • To respond to support enquiries
  • To send occasional product update notifications (you can opt out at any time)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out above:

  • Account data (name, email, TradingView username) — for the lifetime of your subscription, plus up to 2 years thereafter for accounting and customer-service purposes
  • Billing records — for 6 years from the relevant tax year, in line with HMRC record-keeping requirements
  • Terms acceptance log — for 6 years from the end of the subscription, in line with the limitation period for contract claims under the Limitation Act 1980
  • Support correspondence — for up to 2 years after closure of the relevant matter
  • Anonymised analytics — indefinitely, as the data is not personally identifiable

You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 7), subject to our need to retain certain records for legal, tax, or regulatory reasons.

6. Cookies

iotsimalgo.com uses minimal cookies — primarily for analytics purposes only. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR (and equivalent EEA law where applicable to you), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to our retention obligations
  • Restriction — request that we restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office or your local data-protection authority (see Section 11)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@iotsimalgo.com. We will respond within one calendar month, in line with UK GDPR Article 12.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted transport (TLS), restricted access to operational systems, and use of established processors with their own security obligations. Payment processing is handled entirely by Whop and Stripe using industry-standard encryption — we never see or store card details.

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a personal-data breach likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within the timeframes required by UK GDPR Articles 33 and 34.

9. Automated Decision-Making

We do not carry out any automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you under UK GDPR Article 22.

10. Children's Privacy

IOTSIM is not intended for, marketed to, or available to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly.
