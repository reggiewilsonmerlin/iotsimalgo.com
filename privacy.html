Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Applies to iotsimalgo.com and all IOTSIM services

1. Who We Are

IOTSIM is operated by IOTSIM ("we", "us", "our"), reachable at support@iotsimalgo.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit iotsimalgo.com or subscribe to IOTSIM via Whop.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Here is what we hold and why:

Data Source Purpose Basis
Name & email address Whop checkout Account management, subscription comms Contract performance
Payment information Whop / Stripe Billing (we never see card details) Contract performance
TradingView username Provided by you Granting indicator access Contract performance
Pre-checkout question response Whop checkout Confirming you've read the documentation Legitimate interest
Website analytics (anonymous) iotsimalgo.com Understanding site traffic Legitimate interest

We do not collect trading data, account balances, positions, or any information from your TradingView charts. IOTSIM runs entirely inside TradingView's sandbox — no data leaves your chart.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party platforms. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Whop.com — subscription management and payment processing. See Whop Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing via Whop. See Stripe Privacy Policy
  • TradingView — the platform on which the indicator runs. See TradingView Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded on iotsimalgo.com. No personal data is shared

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your subscription and grant access to the indicator
  • To send subscription-related emails (receipts, access confirmations, renewal notices)
  • To respond to support enquiries
  • To send occasional product update notifications (you can opt out at any time)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active, plus up to 2 years thereafter for accounting and legal compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

6. Cookies

iotsimalgo.com uses minimal cookies — primarily for analytics purposes only. No advertising or tracking cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@iotsimalgo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data. Payment processing is handled entirely by Whop/Stripe using industry-standard encryption. We do not store card details.

9. Children's Privacy

IOTSIM is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active subscribers of material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact & Complaints

For privacy-related queries: support@iotsimalgo.com

If you are based in the UK or EU and are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (UK: ico.org.uk).
