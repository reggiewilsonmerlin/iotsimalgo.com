1. Try Before You Pay — 14-Day Free Trial

✓ Every Plan Includes a 14-Day Free Trial

You will not be charged during the trial period.

The trial gives you full access to the Software so you can evaluate whether it suits your needs before any payment is taken. If at any point during the trial you decide IOTSIM is not for you, simply cancel via your Whop dashboard. No charge will be made.

2. UK and EU Statutory Cancellation Rights

If you are a consumer ordinarily resident in the United Kingdom or the European Economic Area, you have a statutory right to cancel a distance-purchased digital service within 14 days of entering the contract — under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013 (UK) and the Consumer Rights Directive (EU).

Our 14-day free trial is designed to coincide with and fulfil this statutory cancellation period. Because no payment is taken during the trial, cancelling within the trial period is the simplest and complete exercise of your statutory cancellation right — no refund is required because no money has changed hands.

If, in any future plan, the trial period is shorter than 14 days or unavailable, your statutory cancellation rights under the above regulations remain unaffected and we will honour them in full.

Nothing in this policy excludes, limits, or affects your non-excludable statutory rights as a consumer.

3. After the Trial — No Refunds

⚠️ All Subscription Fees Are Non-Refundable

Once your free trial ends and your first paid billing period begins, all subscription fees are non-refundable, including for partial billing periods, except where a refund is required by applicable law (such as the statutory rights described in Section 2 or where we have materially failed to provide the Product).

This applies to all plans (monthly, quarterly, annual, Founding Annual, Early Adopter Annual, Early Adopter Quarterly, and any future plan). It applies regardless of:

  • whether you used the Product after billing began;
  • whether you forgot to cancel before the trial ended;
  • whether market conditions or your trading results have changed;
  • whether you changed your mind about your trading approach;
  • whether your circumstances have changed.

We do not pro-rate, partially refund, or credit unused portions of a billing period.

4. How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time via your Whop dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain access to the Product until that period ends, and you will not be billed thereafter.

To cancel during your free trial without charge, you must do so before the trial period ends. We will not retroactively cancel a trial after the first paid billing cycle has begun, and the no-refund rule in Section 3 applies from that point.

If you encounter any technical difficulty cancelling via Whop, contact support@iotsimalgo.com before the end of your billing or trial period and we will assist promptly.
